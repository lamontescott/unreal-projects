 <value> % <modulus>
 Maps the <value> between a range of numnbers
 The range is between 0 and <modulus> - 1

 Ex.
    rand() => 2524
        2524 mapped to a range between 0 - 9
        Not 0 - 10 The range is 0 to the modulus - 1
